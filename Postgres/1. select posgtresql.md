###### Operators to be used with the where clause
- **Equal**: `=`
- **Less than**: `<`
- **Greater than**: `>`
- **Less than or Equal**: `<=`
- **Greater than or Equal**: `>=`
- **Not Equal**: `<>` or `!=`
###### logical operators 
- **AND**
- **OR**
- **NOT**
